Grade 2 Ankle Sprain Response

A Grade 2 sprain involves a partial tear of the ligament. Follow the R.I.C.E. protocol immediately to manage swelling and prevent further damage.

Rest

Avoid weight-bearing activities. Use crutches if moving causes sharp pain.

Ice

Apply cold packs for 15-20 minutes every 2-3 hours for the first 48 hours.

Compression

Wrap the ankle with an elastic bandage. Ensure it's snug but not cutting off circulation.

Elevation

Keep the ankle elevated above the level of your heart as much as possible.

Professional Consult

Grade 2 sprains often require physical therapy. Schedule an X-ray to rule out small fractures.
