Minor Burn

Guidance only
volume_up
Play instructions aloud
Visual identification: Redness without blisters

CHECK FIRST

Ensure the source of the burn has been removed. Check if the burn is larger than the victim's palm or on the face.

DO THIS NOW

  • 1. Run cool (not cold) tap water over the burn for at least 20 minutes.
  • 2. Remove jewelry or tight clothing before the area begins to swell.
  • 3. Apply a loose, sterile bandage or clean cloth.

DO NOT

  • block Do not use ice, butter, or ointments on the burn.
  • block Do not break any blisters that have formed.

WHEN TO CALL EMERGENCY

Dial emergency services immediately if:

Difficulty breathing Deep tissue visible Chemical burn
